Welcome to the New Chargers Stadium

The new home of the San Diego Chargers is a cutting-edge facility designed for the ultimate fan experience. Located next to the iconic Petco Park, this modern stadium combines state-of-the-art amenities with a vibrant, fan-friendly atmosphere. This stadium will be the epicenter of Chargers football for years to come!

Stadium Name: The Chargers Stadium at Petco Park

Capacity: 65,000 seats

Opening Date: September 2025 (Simulated)

Location: Adjacent to Petco Park, San Diego, CA 92101

Stadium Features

  • High-Tech Video Boards - With the largest video screens in the NFL, fans will never miss a moment of the action.
  • Premium Seating Options - From luxury suites to club seating, the stadium offers various premium seating options for the ultimate comfort.
  • Interactive Fan Zones - Experience immersive technologies and virtual reality stations that bring fans closer to the game.
  • State-of-the-Art Sound System - With a next-generation sound system, you’ll feel the intensity of the game like never before.
  • Wi-Fi Connectivity - Enjoy fast, free Wi-Fi throughout the stadium to stay connected, share moments, and check out live stats during the game.

Fan Experience

The fan experience at the new stadium will be second to none. Whether you’re attending a game or enjoying a special event, the stadium is designed to provide an immersive and interactive experience:

  • Interactive Touchpoints: Fans will have access to various interactive screens and kiosks offering live game stats, player profiles, and more.
  • Exclusive Events: Special fan-only events, meet-and-greets with players, and opportunities to explore the stadium’s behind-the-scenes areas.
  • Kid-Friendly Zones: Dedicated family areas featuring kids’ games, face painting, and more fun activities.
  • Gourmet Food & Beverages: The stadium offers a wide range of dining options from local eateries to internationally recognized brands.

Sustainability at the Stadium

Our new home is designed with sustainability in mind. From eco-friendly building materials to energy-efficient lighting, the new stadium sets a new standard for green design in sports venues:

  • Solar Panels: The roof of the stadium will be equipped with solar panels to help power parts of the stadium and reduce its environmental footprint.
  • Water Conservation: Water-saving technologies such as rainwater harvesting systems will help conserve water throughout the stadium.
  • Recycling Initiatives: The stadium will feature comprehensive recycling programs and encourage fans to participate by providing recycling bins throughout the venue.
  • Zero Waste Goals: The stadium is committed to achieving zero waste by minimizing landfill waste through recycling and composting.
