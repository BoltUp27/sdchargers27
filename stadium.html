New Chargers Stadium

The New Chargers Stadium is set to revolutionize the fan experience. Located in the heart of San Diego, right next to Petco Park, this cutting-edge venue will combine world-class amenities with unparalleled views, making it the perfect home for Chargers fans and a destination for all sports enthusiasts.

Stadium Features

The New Chargers Stadium will offer a range of exceptional features that cater to both fans and the team.

  • State-of-the-Art Technology: Large LED screens, advanced sound systems, and free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the stadium.
  • Premium Seating: VIP suites, luxury boxes, and comfortable general seating options for all fans.

The Ultimate Fan Experience

The New Chargers Stadium is designed with fans in mind. Every corner of the stadium is crafted to enhance your game-day experience. Whether you're in the stands, in a suite, or in the fan zone, you'll feel the energy and excitement of being a part of the Chargers community.

Seating Options

The New Chargers Stadium will offer a variety of seating options for every fan:

General Admission

Affordable seating options that provide great views of the field.

VIP Suites

Exclusive, luxury suites offering a premium experience with catered food and private amenities.

Club Seats

Access to VIP lounges and excellent views, with food and beverage service right at your seat.

Sustainability

The New Chargers Stadium is built with sustainability in mind. From the eco-friendly materials used in construction to the energy-efficient systems, this stadium is designed to minimize its environmental impact while maximizing comfort for fans.

  • Solar Panels: The stadium will feature solar panels to help power its lighting and systems.
  • Water Conservation: Water-saving fixtures and rainwater harvesting systems will be in place to reduce water usage.
  • Recycling Initiatives: The stadium will implement a robust recycling program throughout the venue.

Advanced Technology

The New Chargers Stadium will be equipped with cutting-edge technology to enhance the fan experience:

  • Augmented Reality: Experience the game in new ways with AR features in and around the stadium.
  • Interactive Mobile App: A custom app for fans to access stadium maps, food ordering, and exclusive content.
  • Smart Parking: Use the app to find the nearest parking spots and book your parking in advance.
