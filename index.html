Why The Move to San Diego?

After years of competing in Los Angeles, the Chargers made the bold decision to return to their roots in San Diego. The new stadium will offer an incredible fan experience, with cutting-edge technology, top-tier seating, and a vibrant game-day atmosphere.

The New Stadium

Located next to Petco Park, this state-of-the-art venue will be the home for Chargers fans for years to come. It will feature expansive seating options, high-tech amenities, and a breathtaking view of the San Diego skyline.

The Fan Experience

Fans will enjoy more than just the game. With interactive zones, luxury seating, and premium food options, the stadium is designed to keep fans engaged and entertained throughout the day.

Our Commitment to the Community

We're committed to building a stronger community in San Diego, supporting local businesses, and creating unforgettable memories for fans of all ages.
